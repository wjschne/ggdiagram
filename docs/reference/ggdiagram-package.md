# ggdiagram: Object-Oriented Diagram Plots with 'ggplot2'

Creates diagrams with an object-oriented approach. Geometric objects
have computed properties with information about themselves (e.g., their
area) or about their relationships with other objects (e.g, the distance
between their edges). The objects have methods to convert them to geoms
that can be plotted in 'ggplot2'.
